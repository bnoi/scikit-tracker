How to cite scikit-tracker
==========================

scikit-tracker is a young research project. As for now, it's probably to early to submit an article
to a peer-reviewed journal. In the meantime scikit-tracker has been submitted to `Zenodo
<https://zenodo.org>`_ : http://dx.doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.10078.

Zenodo is a webservice that allows researchers and scientists to share publications, posters, movies, softwares, talks and also entire dataset.

So if you use scikit-tracker in a research project, please cite us with the following:

.. epigraph::

    Hadrien Mary et al (2014). Scikit-Tracker : objects detection and robust tracking for cell biology. ZENODO. 10.5281/zenodo.10078
